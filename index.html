Adopt Good Karma

Good Karma Puppy Rescue is a small, local foster-based rescue. We work in the Detroit-metro area and surrounding suburbs to bring adopters together with their loving forever companions. The goal of Good Karma is to never turn away an animal in need, whether through owner surrender, stray rescue or saving animals from kill shelters and puppy mills. Through generous donations and the hard work of our volunteers, we strive to help animals in need and find them loving forever homes.





Team members: Cindy Son, Noemy Hernandez, Marguerite Daniels

Time frame: 3 weeks

Tool: Figma, Illustrator

Overview


Problem:

Adopt Good Karma was designed to find puppies a home. We have observed that our service isnâ€™t obtaining enough traffic, which is causing low adoption rates for puppies. How might we improve Adopt Good Karma so that our puppies are finding good homes based on higher adoption rates?


Solution:

During interviews and surverys, we discovered that potential dog owners were open to adopting a dog as a companion with the exception that it meets certain criteria based on personal preference. Therefore, we believe that having a website that gives enough information on a dog will help these dogs find a good home and that we might be able to help if we implement these search criteria. We might do this by adding detailed descriptions of each dog such as breed, temperament, size, medical history etc. Doing this will allow our organization help dogs find their perfect forever home.


My Role:

Collaborated in the entirity of the project including the ideation phase, creating the style guide, user interviews, interpretting user data, prototyping the low and high fidelity for desktop and mobile.


User Research

Condcuted surveys to find out the users needs and to find solutions to obtain a higher rate of pet adoption.

User Insights

  • Potential dog owners have specific criteria
  • Users need an easy-to-navigate filtering system with a user friendly interface.
  • Users look for comprehensive descriptions to make informed decisions.
  • Regular updates and accurate information.
